About Acuity Drive

Traditional digital mirrors and camera-dependent systems often struggle with glare, focal limitations, and weather-induced distortion, reducing driver confidence. Acuity Drive overcomes these challenges by combining high-accuracy sensors with real-time processing to deliver glare-free guidance, precise distance estimation, and proactive overtaking alerts.

Driver Guidelines

Safety tips from our expert team

  • Always check mirrors and sensors before overtaking.
  • Keep headlights clean and functional during night drives.
  • Maintain safe distance; follow overtaking alerts carefully.
  • Update Acuity Drive software regularly for enhanced performance.
  • Adjust sensor calibration weekly to avoid misreading distances.
  • Report any system anomalies immediately to fleet management.
